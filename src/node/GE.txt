
Role Summary:
Serves as software architect in China to lead the architecture design and development to provide a sustainable, scalable foundation for next generation of IT solutions.
Essential Responsibilities:
• Be accountable for software architecture design and development, and share the responsibility for software deliverables and quality together with IT project management teams
• Evaluate current architecture and define future application development strategy and associated reference architectures with respect to functionality, performance, scalability, reliability, and implementation schedules.
• Drive architecture direction through hands-on implementations, participation in all phases of the development lifecycle along with the development teams through technical guidance and hands-on contributions.
• Creates architecture documentation, including use cases, blueprints, working solutions, technology roadmaps, capability models, etc.
• Assist to ensure high quality of software development with high reliability and scalability by following software quality control mechanism.
• Follow POC approach to research, test and recommend tools, technologies and practices across software design, systems engineering, performance engineering, data management and integration for potential adoption and implementation
• Participate in the domain technical and business discussions relative to future architect direction.
• Assist in the analysis, design and development of a roadmap and implementation based upon a current vs. future state in a cohesive architecture viewpoint.
• Gather and analyze data and develop architectural requirements at project level.
• Participate in the EA (Enterprise Architecture) governance model.
• Support the development software and data delivery platforms that are service-oriented with reusable components that can be orchestrated together into different methods for different businesses.
• Research and evaluate emerging technology, industry and market trends to assist in project development and/or operational support activities.
